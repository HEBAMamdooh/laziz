About the Project

This project was inspired by my personal journey toward leading a healthier lifestyle. As someone who struggled with maintaining a balanced diet and consistent exercise, I wanted to create a tool that could help people stay on track with their health goals. Combining AI-driven recommendations with data from fitness trackers and diet preferences, this web application provides a personalized approach to health and wellness. The project also allowed me to deepen my understanding of web development and machine learning techniques.

This Portfolio Project was developed as part of the Holberton School curriculum. It represents a combination of both my technical and personal growth over the course of the program. You can learn more about the project and contribute through the links below:
